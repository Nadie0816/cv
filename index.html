Briana Atkins

Assistant Designer at Perry Ellis International

The inspiration for my work comes from my interactions with people of other cultures, technology, nature, and photography to give a few examples. The variety in my inspirations and concepts are shown throughout my work. In each project I try to develop my skills and push my abilities further. Fashion should have variety, to change with consumer's mood, and the ability to have an impact on someone's life and wardrobe.
